When learning new things, it's important for us to find the right book to guide us through obscure and complex definitions, and show us the path towards intuitive understanding. Such books for me are Advanced Probability Theory by Bingyi Jing and Probability Theory and Examples by Rich Durrett. With the help of these two, I was able to understand measure, sigma algebra and filtration which are the foundation of advanced probablity theory.
