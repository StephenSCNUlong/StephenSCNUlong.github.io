Statistical Inference by George Casella is the first book I read about randomness. I read through the whole book and finished most of the exercises offered by the book. One of the most fascinating sections is MCMC algorithm, via which we can do distribution sampling indirectly even if we can not do it directly. It made use computer to sample from any distribution a possibility. I would like study more about it.
