I would rather use statistical learning than machine learning to refer to the subject I am talking about, because I think statistical learning is the modernization (using computer to calculate massive data) of statistical inference. Since there are so many notes, videos and websites on the internet, I didn't focus any specific book during the learning process. To name a few, I have used Andrew Ng's lecture videos, Statistical Learning Methods by Hang Li, Machine Learning by Zhihua Zhou and The Elements of Statistical Learning by Trevor Hastie.
